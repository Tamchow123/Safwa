Product, Ops. Review this register at every milestone (Guest Alpha, Core MVP,
each Beta, Launch); no unmitigated High-impact risk may ride into Production
launch.

## Pre-launch gate (must be resolved or accepted in writing before Production launch)

1. Risk 1: redistribution permission confirmed, or a documented decision with
   takedown/replacement plan.
2. Risks 3/4: exclusion tests green; no unverified content reachable by
   learners.
3. Risk 9: Multi-device offline Beta suite green across supported browsers.
4. Risk 11: security pass completed; rate limits verified.
5. Backups restored successfully in a drill (DEPLOYMENT §7).
